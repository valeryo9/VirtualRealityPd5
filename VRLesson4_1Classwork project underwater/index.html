Underwater Experience

Immerse yourself and enjoy a magnificent underwater experience, where you can admire the various animals, plants, and objects found in the depths of the sea—depths that are nearly impossible to explore in real life.

    1. If you want to feel like you're truly underwater and want to hear the pressure of the water, click on a turtle.
    2. If you want to hear the sounds the Kraken makes, click on any Kraken (octopus).
    3. If you want to hear the sounds and cries that whales make, click on any whale.
    WARNING!! This is not a game; it is an experience where you can only see, move, and hear what lies at the bottom of the sea.
